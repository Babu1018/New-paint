About Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that operates without a central authority or banks. It uses peer-to-peer technology to facilitate instant payments.

Created in 2009 by an unknown person using the alias Satoshi Nakamoto, Bitcoin introduced the concept of cryptocurrency to the world.

Bitcoin transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded in a public distributed ledger called a blockchain.
